- Second person ("you", "your") when addressing the reader

- Confident but not pushy — share expertise without hard selling
- Include specific numbers, measurements, and costs when relevant
- Reference real Mississippi geography: Tupelo, Jackson, Hattiesburg, the Gulf Coast, the Delta, DeSoto County, etc.

## Content Structure

Every post MUST follow this structure:

1. **Opening paragraph** (2-3 sentences): Hook the reader with a specific Mississippi scenario or stat. This paragraph should work as the post excerpt.
2. **H2 sections** (3-5): Each covering a distinct subtopic. Use descriptive headers, not generic ones.
3. **H3 subsections**: Break up long H2 sections as needed.
4. **Practical details**: Include specific costs, timelines, measurements where applicable. Use Mississippi-specific data.
5. **Bold key terms** on first use in each section.
6. **Internal links**: Weave 2-3 links to service pages and 1-2 links to related blog posts naturally into the text. Use markdown format: [anchor text](/path).
7. **CTA paragraph**: End with a natural call to action mentioning Smart Roof Pricing. Not salesy — more like "If you're dealing with [topic], we can help. Contact Smart Roof Pricing for a free inspection."
